  • Officer Cupples,
    who was once in our program.

    • Memories for a lifetime.

    Our program is both strict and enjoyable. There will be times when the explorers will go home with a sweat-soaked uniform, or some stinging arms and legs. The Explorers have a great time learning how to prepare themselves for the future. We do everything that we can to help prepare the youthful minds, while also making sure that the program is enjoyable. Many people seem to think that work cannot be fun. We strive to make sure that our students see both sides of the spectrum.
