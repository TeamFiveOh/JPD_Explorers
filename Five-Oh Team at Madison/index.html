    • About the Program

    Law Enforcement Exploring is an educational training program for young adults on the purposes, missions, and objectives of law enforcement. The program provides career orientation experiences, leadership opportunities, and many community service activities.

    The Explorer Program combines classroom training with hands-on instruction. Training can include ride-alongs in police vehicles, front desk duty, firearms training, crime scene processing, handcuffing techniques, arrest procedures, patrol, SWAT tactics, building searches, traffic stops, traffic control, special operations, traffic crash investigation, DUI enforcement, narcotic investigations, and much more.

    In addition to the dynamic training, Explorers can participate in local, regional, and national competitions, campouts, coockouts, social activities, traffic safety, public educational campaigns, and community service projects. Not only this, but you will be able to partake in duties and the life that one in law enforcement would be expected to perform at, what they would do in situations, and how they would carry it out.
