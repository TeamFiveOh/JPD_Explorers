How to Join

Jackson Police Explorers Post 276

  • The beginning application.

    • The Process

    Many requirements must be met to join the explorers; the ability to follow instructions in a timely manner, and the willingness to to carry out the order given. An application, which entails a background check, will be given to the explorer. The application holds a series of questions that have to do with both the explorer and his or her guardians. This information is necessary to ensure the safety of all members in the post.

    Application Process

  1. The explorer will recieve the application.

  2. Upon return, the explorer will be informed on the status of his application.

  3. Arrive to 3 meetings.

  4. Recite "probable cause"(a scripture) by memory.

  5. Recieve your uniform / badge.
