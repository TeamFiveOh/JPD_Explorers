Location

Jackson Police Explorers Post 276

  • Our meeting location, the JPD Complex.

    • Where are we?

    Our meetings are at 234 Institute St, Jackson, TN 38301 each Thursday. Meetings last from 2-2.5 hours, and start at 6:30 PM(1830 Hours). This is the ''command base'' for Jackson's law enforcement, and the JPD Explorers. If you are unable to find the location, search for "Jackson Tennessee Police Department Complex", or dial the phone number at the bottom of the page.
