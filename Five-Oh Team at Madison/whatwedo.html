What We Do

Jackson Police Explorers Post 276

  • Explorers directing traffic at an event.

    • About Our Services

    Our mission is to provide dedicated young people an opportunity to explore the various careers in the criminal justice field. This is achieved by providing them with quality training, challenging hands-on exercises, organized competitions, and the opportunity for developing leadership, maturity, decision making skills, and service to their community.

    Our facilitators provide the details of the event. All other concerns will be discussed in the meetings.

    Our Services

  1. Parking Lot Security
  2. Front Door / Indoor Security.
  3. Parking Guides.
  4. Traffic / Intersection Control
  5. Event foot patrol.
