Post Advisors

Jackson Police Explorers Post 276

    Who are they?

    Our advisors are the foundation of our explorer program. Advisors are the central reason for which all activities are performed. One of their numerous occupations is to ensure all explorers are centered around the job needing to be done. The advisors are the vault of data to all explorers if there is an issue, or a question. Advisors are quite often accessible and are delighted to be of help.
